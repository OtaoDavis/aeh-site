THE CHALLENGE

The core challenge is access to gainful employment.

Africa’s youth employment crisis is not a lack of talent—it is a legacy of constrained models. We address this by reimagining the student-centered university.

Through our holding of premier institutions in Uganda, Zambia, Senegal, and Mali, we inculcate ethical leadership and employability skills from admission to graduation.

OUR SOLUTION

Reimagining Education

01

Holistic Education

We nurture identity, values, and essential life skills to develop grounded, purpose-driven graduates rooted in African culture and ethical frameworks.

02

Apprenticeship-Driven

Learning by doing. We emphasize real-world industry experience, innovation, and collaboration to foster an entrepreneurial mindset.

03

Global Pathways

Connecting students to local and global opportunities, including remote work platforms and international innovation ecosystems.

Our Investors

FinnFund

Finnfund is a Finnish Development Finance Institution (DFI) which builds a sustainable world by investing in responsible and profitable businesses that drive sustainable developments in developing countries.

FinnFund provides businesses operating in developing countries with risk capital, long-term investment loans, mezzanine financing and expertise on how to invest in the developing markets. FinnFund has invested approximately EUR 557 million primarily in clean energy, sustainable forestry, financial institutions, and agriculture. Maarifa is Finnfund’s first investment in the education sector.
