THE IDENTITY

Who Is AEH.

AEH is an education company that owns and operates universities with a unique academic framework that drives superior student outcomes and community impact in Africa.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Globally relevant high-quality education is the most catalytic force for Africa’s innovation, sustainable development, and global competitiveness.

High quality education is the enabler for converting Africa’s bulging youth population into a competitive skilled labor market for the world.

Everyone has the potential to learn, grow and achieve gainful employment and entrepreneurship that contributes positively to their community.

OUR PURPOSE

To develop a New Model of an African University where education and research are fundamentally organized around understanding and solving social and economic problems.

OUR MISSION

To build and scale centers of excellence, innovation and transformation that empower learners to reach their Full Potential in employment and leadership.
